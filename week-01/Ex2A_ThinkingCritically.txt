7. What does all of this suggest about the reliability of the conclusions reached in the article?

Answer:
It suggests that the article’s conclusions may not be fully reliable. Google Trends shows relative search interest, and the results can change depending on which terms are compared and how the search is set up. The data may show some interesting patterns, but it does not clearly prove that these foods are actually the most popular breakup foods.
